# IoT in Real Life: Healthcare Monitoring

## Overview

This project is a case study on the use of the Internet of Things (IoT) in healthcare monitoring. It explains how IoT devices collect and transmit patient health data in real time, helping doctors monitor patients remotely. The report also discusses the applications, benefits, challenges, and future scope of IoT in the healthcare sector.

## Objectives

- Understand the concept of IoT in healthcare.
- Study the working of IoT-based healthcare monitoring systems.
- Explore real-world applications of IoT in healthcare.
- Identify the benefits and challenges of IoT technology.
- Learn about the future scope of IoT in healthcare.

## Applications

- Wearable Health Devices
- Remote Patient Monitoring
- Smart Hospitals
- Medication Management
- Post-Surgery Recovery Monitoring

## Technologies Used

- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Smart Sensors
- Cloud Computing
- Wireless Communication
- Data Analytics

## Conclusion

IoT has revolutionized healthcare by enabling continuous patient monitoring and improving the quality of medical services. Although challenges such as security, privacy, and device reliability still exist, ongoing technological advancements continue to make IoT healthcare systems more efficient and reliable.
